Project Idea

Complete Restructure of the education system.


The project will be aimed at being a complete long-term overhaul of the education system that will
integrate the use of technology within the education systems giving students of any age open access
to any learning materials and testing that the implemented technology understands their level in any
particular area to be at. It will be aimed at using a testing system that still rates the students, but
instead of rating them against their own age brackets it will be aiming to group all people not just
students in areas of comparable knowledge, understanding and personality types.



The true motivation of this idea stems from the simple fact that at some point in the future the world
we live in will be managed more efficiently and effectively, meaning that there will be a point in the
future that every person on the planet is a student at some point in their lives. Effective education
does not have to be restricted to only children and younger students, as university level education
possibilities have advanced over the last two decades significantly, with it the availability to gain
access has improved increasing peoples desires to improve their knowledge through higher learning.



Full internet (self-accessible) profile for everyone on the planet. As profiling is becoming necessary
and is no doubt happening now, it makes more sense to work with the governments to make people
far more confident about profiling, knowing it is for their benefit. The profile is essentially the way
that every individual will have their own AI interactive teaching aid and assistant.

With a completely interactive profile the AI can constantly (second by second) assess the individuals'
complete interactions and reactions. This will also be relevant outside of the education system which
this project is ultimately aiming to become a lifelong interactive learning enabler. With the ability
to assess individuals as they progress through all activities it allows for constant re-adjustments of
the learning materials provided, this can vary from a 8 year old who struggles to write stories, but
can excel naturally at computer programming, to a 48 year old nurse who displays such great
abilities with trouble patients and situations on the wards that her profiling may have started out
when she was 18 as a perfect candidate for nursing but now may indicate she is best suited to be a
foreign relations diplomat (for example only), and in the current world people just do not have the
ability to make such adjustments in their life. Individuals should always be helped move them
through to the most ideal situation in life for themselves.

With what the web provides not only the internet but also with connecting all humans, it will allow for
technology to be not only analyzing each individual current and future but also to match them up with
the world they are In, by that I mean once governments around the world are truly connected and
working together, then it will mean that the technology will automatically know that one individually is
progressing away from one particular field( and the technology will be able to plan it to the second ),
meaning it knows well ahead of time where this particular individual will be placed.

Constant upgrading of everyone provided materials means that everyone will always be being provided
with information and material that is uniquely relevant to their exact and specific point in life,
with regards a lot to do with the AI interaction that will be doing personality assessments 100% of
the time. With technology today and more so in the future to literally have 100% of the planet's
population interconnected through the web of technology as much as the internet then it means as we
become so reliant on technology in our lives the we can also have far greater benefit as 1 population
on this planet. An example of this idea will be as one the nurse moves more towards politics or such
areas then there will be more people and personalities that are lining themselves up to be a perfect
fit for the position needing to be filled, essentially having a world which is 100% positively
monitored in all interactions.

The basis for my product already exists, we have computers whose basic function is to interpret data
and logic, and the concept of fully interactive personal assistants is already a commercial free for all,
with Cortana, Siri, Google's Alexis being the current market leaders. With the idea I have it is less
about the idea of having a PA that you can talk to (that will lead to social issue), I am more aiming
at the idea that we have an internet that is a literal web of connectivity, so I will stay focused on
it being implemented with government policy to protect humans from becoming truly unnecessary by
our own creation.

I think the main moral issue I see with this style of project is that in the future our physical make
up as humans will no doubt have some sort of technological presence, so that is a rather large hurdle
to contemplate before being able to convince the worlds population that for their own future protection,
that governments will take for control over their entire presence and interactions. I think the main
skill needed is a sales type skill to be able to show the world this is necessary to protect us from
the likes of Google.

The outcome I have in mind with this plan being implemented successfully is that there will literally
never be a human on the planet who needs to feel worried about if they have a purpose in the future,
if they are even capable of achieving better things. With the successful implementation of this system
I believe we will finally have true human connectivity, we can take away not just avoid the need for
people to make incorrect decisions because they never had the opportunity or ability or even the
desire (just need) to learn how to make them.
